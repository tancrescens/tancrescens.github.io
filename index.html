About me


I am Crescens, holding a professional certificate in MERN full-stack and a bachelors in Cyber Security.
IT has always been my interest as logic and understanding how things works excites me. Ever wondered how trains could turn without derailing? Or why can't human fly? I've thought of those and it had led me down the path in IT.

With my passion in IT and helping others, I want to create a seamless and enriching world for others. Though I'm not the next Steve Jobs but in the little ways, I want to empower others through making impactful applications.

While taking my Diploma in Information Technology, I was volunteering for an events company and was manning a signup booth. The process of signing up was tedious and manual, naturally being an IT student, I thought of the ways technology could have lightened this burden.
This sparked my fire for automation. I have yet to try out automation but in the near future, I want to veer into the world of automation and enrich people's day to day life.
